2. Improved
3. Negotiated
4. Created
5. Achieved
6. Coordinated
7. Influenced
8. Reduced
9. Established
10. Resolved
11. Accelerated
12. Launched
13 .Pioneered

Verb+ed some verb+ing, resulting/contributing/increasing/decreasing something
F.e. “Improved Azure DevOps CI/CD pipeline, decreasing pipeline runtime by 15 minutes”
